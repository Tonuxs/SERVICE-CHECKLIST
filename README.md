# Service Checklist

A web application designed to simplify the organization and preparation of church services by centralizing all the essential information in one place.

## 📖 About

Preparing a church service often involves coordinating multiple teams, responsibilities, and important details. Information can easily become scattered across messages, notes, and conversations.

**Culto Checklist** was created to solve this problem by providing a simple and practical way to organize everything needed before a service begins.

## ✨ Features

* ✅ Service preparation checklist
* ✅ Worship team organization
* ✅ Speaker information management
* ✅ Important announcements section
* ✅ Equipment verification
* ✅ Task tracking and organization
* 🔜 Service history
* 🔜 PDF export
* 🔜 User accounts and authentication

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal of this project is to improve communication and reduce the chances of forgetting important details during church service preparation.

By keeping all relevant information in a single place, teams can focus more on serving and less on administrative tasks.
